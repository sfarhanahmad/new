Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At PulseGear Pakistan, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or create an account, we collect:

  • Full name and phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Order history and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Communicate order updates via WhatsApp or phone
  • Improve our products and services
  • Send promotional offers (only if you opt in)

3. Data Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud platform. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as needed to process your order (e.g. delivery partners).

4. Payment Information

We do not store any payment details on our servers. Payments via JazzCash or EasyPaisa are processed directly through those platforms. COD requires no online payment.

5. Cookies

Our website may use basic session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us via WhatsApp at 03266570023.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
